Choose Your River Adventure

Whether you want a relaxing scenic float or a high-adrenaline whitewater run, we’ll help you find the perfect trip. Below are a few popular options and what to expect.

Book a Trip

Scenic River Run

Great for families and first-timers. Enjoy calmer water, wide views, and plenty of time to take photos, swim, and relax. Perfect if you want the “outdoors” feeling without the intensity.

  • Best for: Beginners, families
  • Trip length: Half-day or full-day
  • Highlights: Wildlife, swimming spots

Whitewater Rapids

Want the thrill? This is the classic whitewater experience: fast water, big splashes, and heart-pounding drops. Your guide will teach you paddle commands and keep the team working together.

  • Best for: Adventurers
  • Trip length: Full-day
  • Highlights: Rapids, teamwork, excitement

Multi-Day Camping Trip

Make it a full getaway. Combine rafting with overnight camping for a trip that feels like a real expedition. We handle the planning — you enjoy the food, the campfire, and the stars.
